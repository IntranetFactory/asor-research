> Collected: 2026-04-12

## People and agents working hand in hand

The page presents the Workday Agent System of Record (ASOR) as a way to bring AI agents into the same platform used to manage people, with an analytics hub for visibility and accountability.

## What you can do

### Gain complete visibility and total accountability
Workday describes ASOR as a secure way to deploy, extend, manage, and measure agents in one analytics hub for a blended workforce (people plus agents).

Highlighted outcomes include:
- Driving value and ROI from AI investments.
- Uniting AI agents in one hub to reduce agent sprawl.
- Enforcing accountability for safe and compliant data usage.
- Managing an agent lifecycle from registration to retirement.

A partner quote (Accenture Workday Business Group CTO) emphasizes governance and analytics for managing digital workforces at scale.

## Accelerate your agents' value

Workday claims better decisions come from combining agent insights with people and financial data context.

## Enter the Workday Agent Gateway

The page presents Agent Gateway as a secure hub to manage and measure interactions for any agent using Workday data, including third-party agents.
