## Overview

Airtable positions its HR solution as a centralized system for recruiting, onboarding, policy management, and organizational visibility. The core promise is that HR teams can run key people workflows from one configurable platform while integrating with existing tools.

## Core HR Use Cases

### Recruiting Coordination
Airtable emphasizes a shared workflow for hiring plans, interview coordination, and candidate tracking so recruiting stakeholders can work from a common view.

### Onboarding Standardization
The platform highlights repeatable onboarding processes that can evolve as a company scales, with template-driven workflows to accelerate setup.

### Policy and Knowledge Management
Airtable frames HR policy documentation as a single source of truth, reducing fragmented documents and making policies easier to find and update.

### Org Visibility and Planning
Org chart and people data visualization are presented as mechanisms for keeping teams aligned during growth and organizational change.

## Platform Capabilities for HR Teams

Airtable’s HR positioning depends on composability:
- Integrations with common workplace systems.
- Workflow customization through apps and scripting.
- Reusable templates for frequent HR operations.

This approach suggests Airtable is intended less as a rigid HRIS replacement and more as a flexible operating layer for HR workflows that vary by organization.

## Positioning Against HRIS, HRMS, and HCM Categories

Relative to classic system categories, Airtable is best interpreted as a configurable workflow layer rather than a complete HCM suite.
- It can support HRIS-like records and HRMS-like process orchestration when configured appropriately.
- It does not inherently replace country-specific payroll compliance engines in multinational settings.
- In global environments, it is often paired with localized payroll systems while serving as a coordination and visibility layer.

For organizations formalizing AI-agent governance, a separate agent system of record may be used alongside workflow platforms, with clearer controls for agent lifecycle, accountability, and cross-agent analytics.
